### Board Overview
This star-shaped blinky wearable showcases mesmerizing constellation and star art, brought to life by the bright programmable LEDs showcasing dazzling light patterns on a sleek SMD PCB canvas. Powered by the ATtiny13 IC, this wearable features ICSP pins at the bottom, allowing for quick programmability.
