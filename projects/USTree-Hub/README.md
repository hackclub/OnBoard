# USTree-Hub

<!-- Describe your board in 2-3 sentences. What are you making? What will it do? -->
My board is a USB-Hub in the shape of a Christmas tree, in line with the holiday spirit! 
It's called the USTree-Hub, Because it rhymes with USB-Hub and it needed an original name. 
I plan to use this to connect my mouse, phone & powerbank chargers, and USB-flash drives to my laptop. 

<!-- How much is it going to cost? -->
$34.78, according to the screenshot.

<!-- Tell us a little bit about your design process. What were some challenges? What helped? ***Totally optional*** -->
I intially wanted to make a hub with an ethernet port and an SD-card reader as well; 
however, after realising the complexity of the project and the time limit drawing nearer, 
I decided to use only USB-ports, as the tutorial I used was of great help. 
I also thought a tree shape would work well with the concept,
and I added an LED at the tip of the christmas tree, as a cherry-on-top.
